Structs

MeetingSession

A MeetingSession represents an on-going meeting. A meeting session consists of one presenter and zero or more listeners. Each meeting session is given a unique Uuid to differentiate between sessions and to determine which user(s) have priviledges to change meeting session settings, invite attendees, kick attendees, upload presentation slides, etc.
